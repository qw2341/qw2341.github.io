About Me

Welcome to my webpage! I am Qingqi Wu, a passionate and versatile software engineer with expertise in full-stack web development, game development, and artificial intelligence. With a strong educational background in design and development of digital games, complemented by a solid foundation in business administration, I bring a unique blend of technical skills and creative thinking to my work.

Driven by a curiosity to push the boundaries of technology, I am dedicated to creating immersive and impactful experiences for users. I thrive in collaborative environments, leveraging my strong communication and problem-solving skills to work effectively with cross-functional teams.

My journey as a software engineer has allowed me to develop proficiency in a range of programming languages, including Java, JavaScript, Python, and SQL. I am experienced in working with frameworks like Spring, React, and Vue, as well as databases such as PostgreSQL and MySQL.

Beyond coding, I am an avid learner, always seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge and stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends. My passion for continuous growth, combined with my commitment to delivering high-quality work, drives me to create innovative solutions that make a positive impact.
